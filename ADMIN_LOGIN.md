### Step 1: Create Account
1. Click the **"Account"** button in the top navigation bar
2. Click **"Sign Up"** tab

### Step 2: Enter Admin Credentials
- **Name:** Any name (e.g., "Admin User")
- **Email:** Any email (e.g., "admin@simba.rw")
- **Phone Number:** `0788695675` or `+250788695675` ⚠️ **THIS IS THE ADMIN PHONE**
- **Password:** Any password (minimum 6 characters)

### Step 3: Access Admin Panel
After login, you will see:
- **"Admin panel"** button at the bottom-right corner of the screen
- Click it to open the Branch Panel

## Admin Features

### Branch Panel (Branch Operations)
- **Orders Tab:** View and manage all orders, assign to staff
- **Staff Tab:** Monitor staff workload
- **Inventory Tab:** Manage per-branch stock levels
- **Reviews Tab:** View customer reviews, flag inappropriate content

### Admin Panel (Old - Still Available)
- **Dashboard:** Business analytics and KPIs
- **Orders:** Update order status
- **Inventory:** Toggle product availability
- **Customers:** Assign loyalty discounts (0%, 5%, 10%, 15%, 20%)
